# Who we Are

Trust Financial Management Cooperative Society

Trust Financial Management Cooperative Society is a vibrant financial hub designed to support and empower its members through a variety of inclusive financial services. Established in 2018, the society has continually provided a reliable platform where members can grow their savings, access flexible loan packages, and build long-term financial stability. Over the years, it has earned overwhelming credibility through its commitment to transparency, community development, and member satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the foremost promoter of cooperative financial best practices while serving as the financial services provider for all its members across Plateau State.

Our Mission

To provide appropriate mentorship, linkages, and guarantees that will lead to greater access to finance for the sustainable growth and development of its members.

Our Benefits

Our members stand to gain the following:

Financial Inclusion

Ensuring no member is left out of accessible and affordable finance.

Financial Literacy

Empowering members with knowledge to make informed money decisions.

Grassroot Mobilization

Community outreach to engage and empower underserved populations.

Financial Linkage

Connecting members with essential financial opportunities and partnerships.

Capacity Building

Helping individuals and groups build strong, sustainable capacity.

Affordable Finance

Quick and easy access to loans and support tailored for members.

Our Core Strength

Our Core Areas of Strengths include:

1. Membership:

Our membership spans over 5,000, reaching down to the grassroots across Plateau State. We believe this wide coverage will help actualize the Membership Financial Inclusion Strategy, promoting inclusive growth and development.

2. The Cooperative Law:

Our existence and activities are supported by the Cooperative Act of 2004, CAP 98 LFN, and the Cooperative Development Policy for Nigeria (2002).

Geographical Location of Co-operative Societies/Members

The co-operative societies and their members are spread across the 15 Local Government Areas of Plateau State.

Our activities cover key areas of financial inclusion, including access to finance, healthcare, information technology, housing, renewable energy, small and medium enterprises (SMEs), artisans, women, youth, vulnerable groups, and agriculture.
