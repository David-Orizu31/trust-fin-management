Investment & Shares

All the necessary information regarding our investment and shares is outlined below:

1. Membership Eligibility:
  • Must be at least 18 years old (Board members must be 21 years or older).
  • Must share common goals and interests with other members.
  • Should be engaged in an occupation relevant to the primary society’s focus.
2. Registration Requirements:
  • Registration Fee: ₦5,000.
  • Shares Subscription: 25,000 units for ₦50,000 (minimum).
  • Compulsory Monthly Savings: Minimum of ₦5,000.
  • Monthly Dues: ₦300 (for administrative costs)
3. Additional Requirements:
  • Registration fee must be paid within the first month of admission.
  • Members must sign or thumbprint the membership and attendance register.
  • Must meet any additional requirements as specified by the society.
4. Documentation:
  • A letter of intent to join the society
  • Proof of payment for registration and share subscription
  • Any other required supporting documents
